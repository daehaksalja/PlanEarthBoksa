Planearth Architects is a Seoul based architecture firm, founded in 2006 by the architect Park Jun-hyun.

To practice an idea of designing an attractive place for people who work, stay, shop, play and live is a simple identity of our firm.

We provide unique architectural designs in commercial facilities and hotels through integrated process that includes branding, strategy, planning, urban, landscaping, architecture, interior and graphic design.
